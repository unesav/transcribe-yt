About TranscribeYoutubeVideo

Our Mission

TranscribeYoutubeVideo exists to make video content accessible, searchable, and reusable. We believe the knowledge locked inside billions of YouTube videos should be easy to extract and work with — for free.

Whether you're a student taking notes, a content creator repurposing material, a researcher analyzing trends, or someone who simply prefers reading to watching, our tool gives you instant access to the full text of any YouTube video.

What We Do

Our YouTube transcript generator extracts captions from any public YouTube video. Simply paste a URL and receive the complete transcript in seconds. You can:

  • Copy the transcript as plain text or with timestamps
  • Download in SRT, VTT, or TXT format
  • Search for specific words or phrases within the transcript
  • Work with 100+ languages — our tool auto-detects the video's original language

How It Works

When you submit a YouTube URL, our backend retrieves the caption tracks associated with that video. It automatically identifies the best available track — prioritizing creator-uploaded captions and falling back to YouTube's auto-generated subtitles when needed.

The raw subtitle data is then parsed, cleaned, and grouped into readable paragraphs of approximately 60–70 words each, making the transcript easy to scan and work with.

Our Values

  • Free forever — No accounts, no paywalls, no hidden charges
  • Privacy first — We don't store your transcripts or track your usage
  • Accuracy — We always fetch the original language, not a translation
  • Simplicity — Paste a URL, get a transcript. Nothing more, nothing less
